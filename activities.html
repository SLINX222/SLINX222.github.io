Activities at Pacific Trails

Pacific Trails Resort offers a wide variety of activities for guests of all ages and interests. Whether you want to relax and enjoy the scenery or get out and explore, we have something for you:

  • Guided hiking tours along the spectacular California North Coast
  • Ocean kayaking excursions for beginners and experts alike
  • Horseback riding on the beach and through nearby forest trails
  • Evening campfires with storytelling and music under the stars
